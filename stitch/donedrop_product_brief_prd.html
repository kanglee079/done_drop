# DoneDrop Product Requirements & Design Brief ## PRODUCT IDEA DoneDrop is a private social memory app where users capture a real photo moment right after completing something meaningful — a task, habit, challenge, or small personal win — and share it with a private circle (partner, friends, or a small group). ## CORE DIFFERENTIATOR Done → Capture → Share → React → Remember. Emotional goal: “I am really living, progressing, and someone I care about can see it.” ## TARGET USERS 1. Students (motivation/progress) 2. Young professionals (daily wins) 3. Couples/Friends (emotional connection) 4. Accountability circles (study/gym squads) ## VISUAL DIRECTION: "Clean Premium Social" - **Keywords:** Modern, premium, soft, emotionally warm, private, calm, real, elegant. - **Tone:** Quiet confidence, gentle motivation, emotion-first. - **Constraints:** No "productivity app" noise, no Instagram-clone vibe, no childish gamification. ## V1 SCOPE ### Features 1. Onboarding (Use cases: Personal, Couple, Friends, Squad) 2. Create/Mark task as done 3. Photo capture flow (Capture → Caption → Share) 4. Private Circle Feed (Reactions only, no long comments) 5. Personal Memory Wall (Timeline/Archive) 6. Circle Detail & Invite flow 7. Weekly Recap (Emotional highlights) 8. Premium Upsell & Settings 9. Widget integration 10. Comprehensive States (Empty, Loading, Success, Error, Permissions) ### Exclusions - Public feeds, discovery, DMs, video, AI generation, heavy gamification. ## DESIGN SYSTEM GOALS - **Color:** Warm, premium, calming palette with light/dark modes. - **Typography:** Elegant, readable hierarchy. - **Shapes:** Soft corner radii, organic feel. - **Motion:** Smooth, light, elegant; "Signature" Done→Capture flow. - **Implementation:** Flutter-friendly patterns for iOS/Android.
